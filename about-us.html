Who We Are

Antimo is a global trading company focused on commodity markets in the US, Europe and Asia. Our employees encompass a diverse set of backgrounds, areas of expertise and trading styles.

However, the common traits that everyone shares are a proven track record of success and a strong entrepreneurial spirit. Antimo commenced operations in early 2018, but has been able to achieve controlled but very impressive growth due the quality of employees we have been able to attract focused on these principals

Our
History

Founded in 2018 by a small group of commodity traders, Antimo
has grown into well-respected, fast-growing commodity trading firm with a global
footprint.

A subsidiary of Murfin Inc., which also owns Murfin Drilling, Antimo benefits from the stability and reliability of its parent oil-and-gas company.  However, what sets Antimo apart, is the innovative and entrepreneurial spirit of Antimo’s team.

Antimo’s innate ability to think outside-the-box and take calculated risks, combined with the solid backing by Murfin, Inc., have allowed it to capitalize on very unique opportunities since inception and position itself for success in the years to come.
